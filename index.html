This class is a whirlwind tour of the entire world of computing. Topics will include, but are not limited to: fundamentals of computer hardware and software (including operating systems and data storage), networking and internet protocols, database systems, graphics, security, emerging technologies, ethics, and theory of computation. Specific emphasis will be placed on use of Linux command-line interfaces.
